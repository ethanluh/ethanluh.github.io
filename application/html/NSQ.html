2. If you could design a perfect day at school, what would it be? If I could design a perfect day at school, I would have it consist of 2 main subjects so I can focus on learning those subjects thouroughly without my mind wandering of to think about anything else. This way, I can learn more and spend more time learning. Because the attention span for a person is their chronological age plus one, I would have my classes each be around 20 minutes, with a 5 to 10 minute break in between. Since a middle school day is around 7 hours and 10 minutes, I would have 14 class periods per day, meaning that I would have 10 main subject periods and 4 noncollegiate periods, along with 13 five to ten minute breaks and one 20 minute lunch period. If I could only have one day that I designed, I would put my favorite subjects, math(I would probably choose statistics) and ELA.
