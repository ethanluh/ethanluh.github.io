About This Project

The Process

 To code this website, I used two languages; HTML and CSS. The entire website took about three months to code, and I spent a lot of it researching and looking at websites. Mainly, I used two websites called W3Schools and StacksOverflow. W3Schools is a programming "school" and it taught me a lot of the necessary elements of a website. I used StacksOverflow to read other people's questions that related to mine. The rest of the time, I spent coding this website, which took me almost as long.

The Problems

 One of the problems that I ran into was the programming experience. These caused me trouble, because my lack of experience programming made it hard to troubleshoot the problems that I had. So, when I had problems, I resorted to searching up certain topics on W3Schools. This leads me to my next problem, finding the right resources. In the beginning, my website was But, with time, and a little help from my dad, I was able to pull through. Distributing time well was difficult as well because I had so much to do.

The Solutions

 In each scenario, I had a solution at hand. If I had trouble programming a specific part in the website, I looked it up online. When that did not solve my problem, I would go through each and every section in W3Schools. In a way, My computer was a school to me. It provided every resource that a teacher would provide to me at school. But, at home, if you want to learn something on your own, you have to have your own motivation. And the prospect of getting into Nueva, was enough to get me to the end.
