Math

I started math in first grade at Synapse School. Synapse wasn't the most mathematically advanced school, and I didn't really enjoy it. So, last year, when I transfered to Bullis, math was a little more difficult. However, with a bit of time, completing homework and learning became steadily easier. During summer break, I enrolled in CTY, a three week summer camp that focuses on a specific topic. I chose to do Data and Chance, and in three weeks, I learned a lot. When the camp ended, my love for math had strengthened. This school year, I am doing Euler Circle, a extracirricular activity that focusus on number theory. There, I learned the foundations of math, how to prove my conjectures, and advanced my mathematical knowelege. However, after all is said and done, I think CTY was my favorite, because it was a mix of understanding and doing.
