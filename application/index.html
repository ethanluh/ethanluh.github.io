Personal Statement:

My name is Ethan Luh and I am 12 years old. I enjoy fencing, mathematics, and Igo(Go). I want to go to Nueva because of the following two reasons. I need a school where I can mathematically advance my knoledge, and I need a school that can support my love of fencing. During primary school, I went to Synapse School. Synapse was able to support my love of fencing; in fact, they let me travel whenever I wanted. However, at Synapse School, I was not able to learn mathematics as well. For the past two years, I have been going to Bullis Charter School. Bullis was able to help build a strong foundation for my math skills, but couldn't let me travel as much for fencing. To me, Nueva can allow me to do both of my passions. I can learn mathematics, hit my ceiling, and break through it.
